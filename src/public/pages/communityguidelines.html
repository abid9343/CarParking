Community Guidelines

Share only photos and videos that you took or have the right to share.



Intellectual property rights:


users should only post the content that they own on NEEO Platforms. Always share the pictures and posts that you own.

Crime, bullying, selling and buying:


NEEO does not tolerate hate groups, terrorism, and violence. Any post containing support to such should be reported to NEEO and will be deleted from the Platform. Users and accounts promoting such acts will be permanently banned and removed from NEEO Platforms. Selling and buying tobacco, alcohol, firearms, and drugs, as well as sexual services, is prohibited on NEEO Platforms.


Selling guns is also prohibited on NEEO Platforms.


Content that is targeted to private individuals such as but not limited to, hate speech, cyberbullying, shaming, harassment, and blackmail is prohibited on NEEO. If you are concerned and want to bring attention to a particular issue, please report here Contact Us.

Nudity and pornography:


Nudity is not allowed on NEEO. For varying reasons, NEEO prohibits users from posting Nude content on the Platform. Users from varying cultures and ages use our application, posts should be appropriate for a diverse audience. If you think that a user violates these guidelines, please report here. (link should be attached).


Be thoughtful of your posts:


NEEO is a platform to communicate and connect with different individuals from various parts of the world. While sharing posts on NEEO and communicating with others, make sure to respect everyone. Make sure to keep our community secure and safe for people from all backgrounds and ages.
NEEO does not tolerate attacks or discrimination towards anyone regardless of their religion, national background, race, ethnicity, sex, gender identity, gender, disabilities, sexual orientation, or diseases.
